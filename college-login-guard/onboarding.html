Your Shield Against
Phishing Attacks

Fake login pages steal thousands of student credentials every year. This extension watches every page you visit and alerts you the moment something looks wrong.

1

How It Protects You

Powerful layers of defence run automatically on every page you visit.

🌐

Domain Verification

Checks if the site belongs to your college's official domains instantly.

🔍

Typosquat Detection

Catches fake domains that look almost identical to real ones (e.g. kct-login.com).

🔒

SSL Analysis

Grades the site's HTTPS certificate and flags suspicious or missing SSL.

📅

Domain Age Check

New domains (under 90 days old) are a major phishing signal — we flag them.

2

Understanding Verdicts

Every page gets one of three verdicts shown in the popup and badge.

✅

Safe

Official domain, trusted IP, valid SSL. Proceed normally.

⚠️

Warning

Something looks off. Double-check before entering any passwords.

🚨

Danger

High confidence fake page. Leave immediately — do NOT log in.

A warning banner appears in-page and a browser notification fires for Warning and Danger verdicts.
